# Evaluation & Testing

Evaluation and testing for AI systems covers the methods and infrastructure
needed to assess whether AI agents and models perform correctly in
production. This includes offline evals, online evals, persona evals,
and observability for non-deterministic systems.

Key themes from AI Engineer World's Fair 2026:

- **Production evals** — Building eval pipelines for agentic AI systems
- **Persona evals** — Why persona-based evals can be misleading
- **Deterministic infrastructure** — Building reliable infrastructure for non-deterministic agents
- **Reproducibility** — The challenge of reproducing production failures
