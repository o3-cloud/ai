# Production AI Systems

Production AI covers the infrastructure, processes, and systems needed
to deploy AI in real-world environments — from government to enterprise.

Key themes from AI Engineer World's Fair 2026:

- **Government AI** — Shipping production AI inside government
- **Enterprise scaling** — Building and scaling AI assistants for enterprise
- **Financial compliance** — Multi-document correlation for fraud detection
- **ETL pipeline failures** — RL-based detection and remediation
