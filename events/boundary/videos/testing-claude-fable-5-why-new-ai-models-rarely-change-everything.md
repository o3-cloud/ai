# Testing Claude Fable 5: Why New AI Models Rarely Change Everything

**Speaker:** Dex & Vaibhav
**Company:** Boundary
**Channel:** Boundary
**Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hTkmSVuDMPg

## Summary

This episode puts Claude Fable 5 through hard engineering problems instead of benchmarks. The takeaway is that real debugging sessions are often better model evaluations than synthetic tests, and that most new models deliver incremental rather than revolutionary gains.

## Key Points

1. Real engineering problems are better benchmarks than synthetic tests.
2. Model improvements are often incremental, not magical.
3. Painful debugging sessions reveal more than leaderboards do.
4. Context matters when evaluating whether a model changed your workflow.
